aws / cloud computing

Cloud computing is a technology that allows you to access and use a variety of computing resources (such as servers, storage, databases, networking, analytics, machine learning, and more) over the internet, often referred to as "the cloud." Cloud computing eliminates the need for organizations and individuals to own and maintain their own physical data centers and servers, providing a more flexible and cost-effective way to scale and manage IT resources.
